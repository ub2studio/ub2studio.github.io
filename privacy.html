⚡ The Short Version

UB2 Studio apps do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information. All your data lives only on your phone. Our apps display ads through Google AdMob, which uses your device's Advertising ID. That's it.

Section 01

About UB2 Studio

UB2 Studio is an independent Android app development studio based in Mumbai, India. We develop and publish mobile applications for the Google Play Store across categories including finance utilities, productivity tools, and entertainment.

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications developed and published by UB2 Studio, both current and future releases.

Section 02

Information We Do Not Collect

We have designed our apps to minimize data collection. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, phone number, or email address
  • Financial details you enter (salary, expenses, savings, investments)
  • Account credentials, login information, or registration data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Contact lists, photos, files, or messages from your device
  • Location data (precise or approximate)

We do not share, sell, or transfer any personal data to third parties because we do not collect it in the first place.

Section 03

Information Stored on Your Device

Some of our apps store data locally on your device for app functionality. This includes:

  • Saved goals, plans, calculations, and preferences
  • App settings and theme preferences
  • Cached content for offline use

This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data.

Section 04

Advertising — Google AdMob

Some UB2 Studio apps display advertisements via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect anonymous, device-level data — primarily the advertising ID (a resettable identifier provided by Android) and approximate country — to serve relevant ads, per Google's standard advertising policies.

The advertising ID is not linked to your personal identity. You can:

  • Reset your advertising ID at any time via your device's Settings → Privacy → Ads → Reset advertising ID
  • Opt out of personalized ads via Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of ads personalization
  • Review Google's AdMob privacy practices at policies.google.com/technologies/ads
Section 05

Data Deletion Requests

Since UB2 Studio apps do not collect personal data, there is generally nothing to delete from our servers — we don't have any servers storing your data.

However, if you wish to request deletion of any data associated with your device's advertising ID, please contact us at contact@ub2studio.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". We will acknowledge your request within 7 days and process it within 30 days.

For data stored locally on your device by our apps, you can delete it instantly by uninstalling the app.

Section 06

Reference Information Sources

Our finance-related apps reference publicly available data from official Government of India sources. We do not have access to any private or restricted government databases — all information used is openly published. Users are strongly encouraged to verify current rates, rules, and regulations directly from these official sources before making any financial decisions:

  • Income Tax Department: incometax.gov.in
  • Union Budget Information: indiabudget.gov.in
  • National Savings Institute (PPF): nsiindia.gov.in
  • Reserve Bank of India: rbi.org.in
  • Securities and Exchange Board of India: sebi.gov.in
