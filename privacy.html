Policy

How UB2 Studio handles your data — across all our apps.

Last updated: 20 May 2026 Applies to all UB2 Studio apps Mumbai, India
⚡ The Short Version

UB2 Studio apps do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information. All your data lives only on your phone. Our apps display ads through Google AdMob, which uses your device's Advertising ID. That's it.

01 — Identity

Who we are

UB2 Studio is an independent Android app development studio based in Mumbai, India. We build and publish applications on the Google Play Store including FinCalc India Pro, Punjee, Kharchify, and more.

Questions? Reach us at contact@ub2studio.com

02 — Data Collection

Data we collect

We do not collect any personal data. Our apps do not require account registration, login, or any personal information to function.

All data you enter — calculations, subscriptions, goals, preferences — is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

03 — Advertising

How we show ads

Our free apps display advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID to personalise ads. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalised ads in your Android settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

04 — Storage

Where your data lives

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard Android storage (Hive local database). We have zero access to this data. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data.

05 — Third Parties

Third-party services

We use Google AdMob for in-app advertising. No other third-party analytics, crash reporting, or user tracking services are integrated into our apps.

06 — Children

Children's privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

07 — Updates

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our apps or legal requirements. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Have a question?

We're happy to help. Reach us at contact@ub2studio.com and we'll respond within 48 hours.
